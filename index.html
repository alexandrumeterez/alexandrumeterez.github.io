I'm currently a Data Science MSc. student at ETH Zürich, advised by Prof. Gunnar Rätsch. Previously, I was a BSc. student at Politehnica University of Bucharest under the supervision of Prof. Iuliu Vasilescu. During my studies I have completed internships at IBM Research and Adobe. I am mainly interested in deep learning theory and the mathematical foundations of AI, as well as the applications of AI to healthcare. My main research interests are in:

  • Theory: Deep Learning Theory, Optimization, Theory of Foundation Models

  • Applications: Bioinformatics, AI in Healthcare

In my free time, I like to tinker with various technologies, read and ride or fix my bike.
